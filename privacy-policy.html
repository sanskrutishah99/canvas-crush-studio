Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

1. Who We Are

Canvas Crush Studio ("we", "our", "us") operates canvascrushstudio.com. We curate and sell original artworks and fine art prints. This policy explains how we handle your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address — when you sign up for our newsletter or place an order
  • Shipping address — to deliver your artwork
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe; we never store your card details
  • Browser data — standard analytics (pages visited, time on site) to improve your experience

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • To send our newsletter (only if you opted in — you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To improve our website and curated collections
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your cart contents and preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect cart functionality.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Shipping carriers — to deliver your order
  • Cloudinary — for image hosting

All partners are bound by strict data protection agreements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@canvascrushstudio.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain order information for 7 years for tax and legal purposes. Newsletter subscriber data is kept until you unsubscribe. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

8. Security

We use industry-standard SSL encryption for all transactions. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at:
Email: hello@canvascrushstudio.com
Website: canvascrushstudio.com
